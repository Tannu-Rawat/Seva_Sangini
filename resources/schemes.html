Resources

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

National scheme
Cash benefits for pregnant women and lactating mothers to improve health seeking behavior
Benefits: ₹5,000 in three installments for first live birth. Additional incentive for second child if girl (PMMVY 2.0)
Eligibility:
  • Pregnant women (first child, and second if girl child under PMMVY 2.0)
How to Apply: Through Anganwadi worker with MCP card, Aadhaar card, and bank account details

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

National scheme
Promotes institutional delivery among poor pregnant women
Benefits: Cash assistance for institutional delivery (₹1,400–₹2,000 in rural, ₹1,000–₹1,400 in urban areas, plus ASHA incentives)
Eligibility:
  • All pregnant women in government health centers (in low-performing states)
  • BPL/SC/ST women in accredited private institutions
How to Apply: At government health centers or through ASHA worker

Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakram (JSSK)

National scheme
Free and cashless services for pregnant women and sick newborns up to 1 year
Benefits: Free delivery (including C-section), free drugs, diagnostics, diet, blood, and transport for pregnant women and sick infants
Eligibility:
  • All pregnant women and sick newborns up to 1 year in public health institutions
How to Apply: At government health facilities

Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)

National scheme
Assured antenatal care on the 9th of every month
Benefits: Free, quality antenatal check-ups by specialists for all pregnant women, especially high-risk pregnancies
Eligibility:
  • All pregnant women
How to Apply: Visit designated government health facilities on the 9th of each month

Surakshit Matritva Aashwasan (SUMAN)

National scheme
Assured, dignified, respectful, and quality healthcare at no cost for every woman and newborn
Benefits: Zero tolerance for denial of services, free healthcare for pregnant women, mothers up to 6 months post-delivery, and all sick newborns up to 1 year
Eligibility:
  • All pregnant women, mothers (up to 6 months after delivery), and newborns (up to 1 year)
How to Apply: At public health facilities

Labour Room Quality Improvement Initiative (LaQshya)

National scheme
Improves quality of care in labour rooms and maternity operation theatres
Benefits: Ensures respectful and quality care during delivery and immediate postpartum period
Eligibility:
  • All pregnant women delivering in public health facilities
How to Apply: At government health facilities

Dr. Muthulakshmi Maternity Benefit Scheme (Tamil Nadu)

State scheme (Tamil Nadu)
Financial and nutrition support for poor pregnant mothers
Benefits: ₹18,000 in 5 installments plus nutrition kit (health mix, IFA syrup, dates, biscuits, ghee, etc.)
Eligibility:
  • Poor pregnant mothers, age 19+, up to two deliveries
How to Apply: Register pregnancy before 12 weeks at local health center (VHN/UHN/PHC/UPHC)

Home Based Newborn Care (HBNC)

National scheme
Incentivizes ASHA workers for home visits to newborns up to 42 days
Benefits: ASHA visits for weight monitoring, vaccination, safety of mother and newborn, and birth registration
Eligibility:
  • All newborns up to 42 days after birth
How to Apply: Through ASHA workers

Home Based Care for Young Child (HBYC)

National scheme
Extension of HBNC for children 3–15 months
Benefits: Home visits by ASHA/Anganwadi to promote breastfeeding, complementary feeding, immunization, and early childhood development
Eligibility:
  • Children aged 3, 6, 9, 12, and 15 months
How to Apply: Through ASHA/Anganwadi workers

Facility Based Newborn Care (FBNC)

National scheme
Special newborn care units (SNCU), stabilization units, and care corners in hospitals
Benefits: 24x7 care for sick and small newborns, including low birth weight, immunization, and follow-up
Eligibility:
  • All sick and small newborns in public hospitals
How to Apply: At district/sub-district hospitals and medical colleges
