Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

National scheme
Cash benefits for pregnant women and lactating mothers to improve health seeking behavior
Benefits: ₹5,000 in three installments for first live birth
Eligibility:
  • pregnant women
  • first child only
How to Apply: Apply through Anganwadi worker with MCP card, Aadhaar card and bank account details
