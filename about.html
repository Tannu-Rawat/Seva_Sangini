About Seva Sangini

Seva Sangini is a digital companion for ASHA workers and healthcare volunteers, designed to simplify daily healthcare management and empower communities.

  • Track maternal & child health efficiently
  • Identify high-risk cases instantly
  • Access schemes & medical guidelines easily
  • Reduce paperwork and manual errors

Why We Made This Website

We created Seva Sangini after seeing the daily struggles of frontline health workers-juggling paperwork, missing follow-ups, and lacking quick access to vital information. This platform solves:

  • Centralizing patient data for quick retrieval and action
  • Automating alerts for urgent/high-risk cases
  • Providing a one-stop dashboard for all daily tasks
  • Empowering ASHA workers with digital tools for better outcomes
