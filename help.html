Frequently Asked Questions

Who can call the helpline?
Any SEWA Sangini member, parent, or community member needing assistance with our services.
What support can I get?
Information on child care, caregiver training, enrollment, health services, and reporting issues.
Is my information confidential?
Yes, all your queries and concerns are handled with strict confidentiality and respect.
How long does it take to get a response?
We aim to respond to calls immediately during working hours and reply to emails within 24 hours.
